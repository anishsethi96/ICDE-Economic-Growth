Government consumption of Belgium increased sharply in the late 1900 and dropped suddenly because of the goverment beginning to adopt the quite powerful Euro in 2000.
GDP per capita also increased sharply in the 21st century. A vast majority of Belgium women are employed in the service sector and a meagre 4% of the population (Male and Female) are employed in the agriculture sector.
About 60% and 40% Males are employed by the service and industry sector respectively.

Primary School enrollement in males is slightly higher than females and generally decrease with time. Secondary and Tertiary schools see more females enrolling than males.

Note: Enrollment in Secondary and Tertiary school inceases with time wheare as primary school decreases.
